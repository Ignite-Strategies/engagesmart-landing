Use of Information Received from Google APIs

EngageSmart's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Consent first

We connect to Google APIs (Gmail, YouTube, Google Ads) only when you explicitly authorize an integration.

Scope minimization

We request the minimum scopes needed for the feature you enable.

Purpose limitation

We use Google-sourced data only to provide user-facing features visible in EngageSmart (e.g., send a campaign you initiate; show analytics you request).

No selling / limited sharing

We do not sell Google-sourced data. We do not share it with third parties except processors required to operate the service (under contract) or when you direct us to (e.g., publish a video) or as required by law.

No targeted ads using your Google data

We do not use Google-sourced personal data to build interest profiles or for ad targeting.

No training generalized AI models on your Google data

Unless you explicitly authorize it and we clearly disclose it.

Security

  • Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Token storage and other sensitive data encrypted at rest
  • Least-privilege access controls

Disconnect & deletion

Disconnect any Google integration in Account → Integrations; delete your account/data in Account → Delete Account or email support@engagesmart.app.
