About EngageSmart

Born in F3. Built for community impact.

Our Story

We started in F3—a nationwide network of free, peer-led men's workouts. What began as fitness became something deeper: a way to build community, develop leaders, and accelerate impact.

As we ran events—fundraisers, volunteer days, challenges—we realized something critical: these weren't just fundraisers. They were community accelerators.

But we had a problem. The tools we needed didn't exist. We were stuck juggling spreadsheets, Gmail threads, and disconnected platforms—trying to answer one simple question: Who showed up? Who engaged? And who's ready to step up next?

So we built EngageSmart.

Our Mission

Help community-led organizations measure belonging and turn it into action.

Because momentum matters more than moments. And when you measure what matters, you can build something that lasts.

What We Believe

Community > Customers

The best organizations don't build customer lists. They build communities. We help you treat people like humans, not contacts.

Engagement > Transactions

A donation is just a moment. Engagement is momentum. We help you build relationships that compound over time.

Impact > Metrics

Vanity metrics lie. We help you measure what actually matters: belonging, action, and lasting change.
