1) What EngageSmart does

We help community-led orgs capture attendees, follow up with meaning, and measure engagement across events and outreach.

3) How we use data

  • Operate the service: CRM timelines, messaging, actions, analytics.

4) Use of information received from Google APIs

When you connect Google services, we use your data only to deliver user-facing features you request (visible in the UI).

Examples:

  • Gmail: draft/send outreach you initiate; read delivery/label metadata for performance.
  • YouTube: read channel/video metadata; optionally upload/schedule content you request.
  • Google Ads: read account/campaign performance; optionally create/update campaigns you configure.

Security & limits: data is encrypted in transit; sensitive credentials (e.g., OAuth tokens) encrypted at rest; least-privilege access. We do not sell Google-derived data, and we do not share it with third parties except processors needed to run the service (under contract) or when you explicitly direct us to, or as required by law.

EngageSmart's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.
